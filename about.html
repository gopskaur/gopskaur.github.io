Ideate, create, validate, repeat.

I'm a digital product designer who helps brands bring their ideas to life in beautiful, smart and purposeful ways. Putting these values to practice, I'm currently working at DPD Group to create user interfaces, prototypes and design libraries for their internal and external platforms.

Previously, I had the opportunity to work with John Lewis to contribute an array of User Interface designs for their latest internal company app.

I started my journey in digital design back in secondary school where I studied Graphic Design and fell in love with creating things using a computer. This journey continued to flow throughout the years by experimenting with softwares and new technologies to create useful visual design solutions.

I would tell you about the things that inspire me, but I'd rather show you. You can also take a look at my CV to find out more about my experience and the things I aspire to achieve.
