I'm a designer currently living in Birmingham, England.

I'm a visual designer who helps brands bring their ideas to life in beautiful, smart and purposeful ways. Putting these values to practice, I'm currently working with John Lewis to create User Interface designs for their latest internal company app.

I would tell you about the things that inspire me, but I'd rather show you. You can also take a look at my CV to find out more about my experience and the things I prosper to achieve.
