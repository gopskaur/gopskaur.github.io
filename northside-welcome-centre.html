Northside Welcome Centre

The Northside Welcome Centre serve their local community and beyond by providing life advice, musical concerts as well as a range of classes to keep everyone educated and help them to socialise with others.

In order to help market the community centre as well as their activities and ethics, the volunteers and community members at Northside wanted a rebrand and new website to be designed and developed.

To kick the process off, the current site's information architecture was rearranged. Once this was done, the design process was started by creating a new logo, style guide and visual page layouts. Then following through to initialising the new website's development.

Northside's homepage was designed using Sketch and developed using Bootstrap. You can take a look at the progress of the responsive homepage by clicking the link below.
