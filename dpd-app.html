DPD App

The DPD App redesign all started back in early 2018 and is continuing with ideas to evolutionise the courier industry.

I solely started working on the startup aspects of the app, later on joined by a Senior Product Designer to collaborate and flush out mutliple functions including the base navigation structures, layout and theme style.

Some examples of the user interfaces I worked on includes App verification, Delivery preferences and Pickup shops.

We worked from sprint to sprint with guidance from functionality diagrams created by Solution Architects to produce detailed UI flows and prototypes for the Startup, Homepage, Pickup Shop, History, Settings and Notification sections.

The early stages of the project designs were created using Sketch, however in late 2019 DPD Design moved on to using Figma. Since then I have been the main gatekeeper of the design system for this application whereby components, colours, typography and iconography libraries are collated.
