Rosana Pina

Rosana Pina is a graphic designer who was looking to set up her portfolio online to promote the web and print based artwork she creates.

Collaborated with Rosana to help design and build an online portfolio to showcase the recent projects which she has been working on.

In November 2016, I worked alongside Rosana to sketch and design the entire her portfolio website using Photoshop and Illustrator. Then moved onto individually developing the website responsively using Bootstrap.

During this project I learn't how to boost my responsive development workflow and advance my CSS skills to create user-friendly and content wise layouts.
